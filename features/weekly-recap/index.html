A weekly recap that keeps the thread

When you’re busy (or alone a lot), weeks blur. Mallo’s weekly recap is a gentle reflection—what mattered, what was heavy, what kept repeating—so your life doesn’t feel like it resets every Monday. It’s powered by memory, so the recap connects to your real threads, not generic platitudes.

What a good weekly recap includes

  • Wins you might otherwise dismiss.
  • Weight (stress, uncertainty, tiredness) without judgment.
  • Threads you’re building—so the story continues.
