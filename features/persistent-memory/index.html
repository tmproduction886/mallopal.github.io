Persistent memory that remembers you

Most chatbots feel disposable: you open a blank screen and re-explain your life. Mallo is built around persistent memory so your companion can keep the thread—your people, your season, your ongoing story—across days and weeks.

Why memory changes how it feels

  • Continuity: your check-ins don’t reset every day.
  • Less effort: you don’t have to repeat yourself to feel understood.
  • More real: the relationship can deepen instead of staying novelty-driven.

Privacy and boundaries

Mallo is not therapy and doesn’t make clinical claims. And we don’t sell conversation data to advertisers. For details on data handling and controls, see Privacy and Security.
