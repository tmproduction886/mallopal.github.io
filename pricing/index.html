Mallo · Pricing

Mallo pricing (free to start)

Mallo is free to start on iPhone. Some features are available via optional subscriptions. Because App Store pricing can vary by country and can change, the most accurate pricing is always shown inside the app at the time of purchase.

What you get with Mallo

  • Daily check-ins that are low-pressure (2–5 minutes).
  • Persistent memory so the thread continues across days and weeks.
  • Weekly recap that reflects what mattered and what’s recurring.
  • Optional voice and photo features (where available).

How subscriptions work

Subscriptions are purchased through the Apple App Store (and managed by Apple). Subscriptions typically auto-renew unless cancelled. You can manage or cancel in iOS Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions.

For details on data and billing-related processing, see Privacy and Terms.
