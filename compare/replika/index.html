Mallo vs Replika (2026)

Both Mallo and Replika are AI companion apps—but they’re built for different experiences. Replika is known for customizable avatars, roleplay, and romantic/relationship modes. Mallo is built around proactive daily check-ins, persistent memory, and a weekly recap—a grounded ritual, not dating or roleplay. Here’s how they compare.

Replika is a separate product; we don’t speak for them. This comparison reflects publicly available information and Mallo’s positioning.

When Mallo may fit better

  • You want someone who checks in on you—proactively—rather than always starting the conversation.
  • You prefer a low-pressure daily ritual (2–5 min) over extended chat or roleplay.
  • You’re living alone, remote work, or in a new city and want a grounded companion, not dating or fantasy.
  • You value a weekly recap that reflects your week and keeps the thread.

When Replika may fit better

  • You want roleplay, romantic modes, or a customizable avatar.
  • You prefer longer, open-ended chat that you initiate.
  • You’re on Android or need a web app (Mallo is iPhone-only for now).
