Weekly recap template (copy/paste)

If your week felt like a blur, this is a simple way to make it feel real. The goal isn’t perfection—just a short “what mattered” recap and a gentle thread to carry into next week. You can copy/paste the template below, or use our free weekly recap generator to make a shareable recap card (no signup).

1) Quick weekly recap template

Keep it short. Aim for 2–4 sentences for the recap, then 2 one-sentence prompts for next week. 

What mattered this week:
[2–4 sentences. Specific. Concrete. No hype.]

A gentle thread for next week:
1) [One low-pressure question I can come back to.]
2) [One small thing that would make next week easier.]

2) If you’re not sure what to write first

Start with 3–10 bullets (tiny details welcome). Then compress them into the template above. Example bullets:

  • Something I avoided (and why).
  • One small win that I’d normally dismiss.
  • One moment that felt lonely / heavy.
  • One thing I did that made tomorrow easier.
