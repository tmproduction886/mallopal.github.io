All articles are **human-written**, empathetic, and aligned with Mallo’s ICP (living alone, relocated, remote work, high-stress). They target long-tail search and support the app CTA without being pushy.

## Seasonal vs location

- **Seasonal:** The holiday article is relevant from late November through early January (Thanksgiving, Christmas, New Year). It avoids toxic positivity and speaks to people who are alone, far from family, or simply not in the festive mood. Reuse or lightly refresh each year; consider a “winter blues” or “February reset” variant later if needed.
- **Location:** The “first week by region” article is location-specific in content (US, UK, elsewhere) but one URL. It links to the generic [7-day guide](/tools/guide.html) and adds what changes by region (paperwork, norms, pace of connection). No city-level pages yet—regions keep quality high and maintenance low.

## SEO

- Each article has: unique `<title>`, meta description, keywords, canonical URL, Open Graph, Twitter Card, and Article JSON-LD.
- Internal links: to homepage, to `/tools/guide.html` where relevant, and to other articles in the footer.
- Sitemap includes all article URLs.

## Cross-links

- Homepage has an **Articles** section (`#articles`) with three cards linking to these articles.
- Each article footer links to the other two (and Privacy/Terms) for discovery and SEO.
