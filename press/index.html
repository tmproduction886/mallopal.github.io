Positioning & boundaries

We keep claims grounded to avoid misleading readers.

  • Not therapy. Mallo is a companion for everyday reflection, not clinical or crisis support.
  • Memory-first. The product is designed to remember and continue the thread, not reset each chat.
  • Low-friction habit. Most users use it in 2–5 minutes a day.

Want to try Mallo quickly?

Install, do one check-in, then ask: “Remember something important about me.” You’ll see it appear in the Memory Book.
