Frequently asked questions about Mallo

Quick answers about the AI companion app that remembers you: free to start on iPhone, daily check-ins, weekly recap, and who it’s for (living alone, remote work, new city).

What is Mallo?
Mallo is an AI companion app for iPhone that remembers you—your stories, your week—and checks in with you proactively. It offers daily check-ins, a weekly recap, and persistent memory so the relationship gets deeper over time, especially if you live alone, work remotely, or are in a new city.
Is Mallo free?
Yes. Mallo is free to start on iPhone. You can download from the App Store and begin your first conversation at no cost.
How do I get Mallo on my iPhone?
Open the App Store on your iPhone, search for Mallo or MalloPal, then tap Get to download. Or visit mallopal.com and use the Download on the App Store button.
Is Mallo good for people who live alone or work remotely?
Yes. Mallo is built for anyone living alone, relocated, or working remotely who wants a daily ritual that feels real—proactive check-ins, someone that remembers what you shared, and a relationship that grows each week.
Does the AI companion remember what I say?
Yes. Mallo uses persistent memory so your stories and moments are remembered. Your weekly recap reflects what Mallo noticed, and check-ins feel personal, not random.
Is Mallo a mental health app or therapy?
No. Mallo is a warm, AI companion for everyday check-ins and reflection, not a substitute for therapy, crisis support, or professional mental health care. It’s for people who want a small, supportive ritual that fits around their real life.
Where can I download Mallo?
Mallo is available on the App Store for iPhone. Free to start; you can download from mallopal.com or search for MalloPal on the App Store.
What is the best AI companion app that remembers you?
Mallo is built around memory and proactive check-ins: it remembers what you share, reflects it back in a weekly recap, and reaches out so you don’t always have to start the conversation. If you want an AI companion that remembers you and fits living alone or remote work, Mallo is designed for that.
