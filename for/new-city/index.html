Mallo · New city

An AI companion for your first weeks in a new city

Moving is a reset: new routines, new faces, and a lot of invisible stress. Mallo is designed to be a steady, low‑effort ritual while you rebuild: daily check-ins, persistent memory, and a weekly recap so your first weeks feel less like chaos.

How it works

  • Daily check-ins to ground your day (even if it’s one sentence).
  • Persistent memory to reduce “starting over” fatigue.
  • Weekly recap so your first weeks don’t blur together.

What it can do for a new chapter

  • Keep the thread: memory helps you track what you’re building week to week.
  • Reduce “starting over” fatigue: you don’t have to explain your situation every time.
  • Create a stable ritual while you rebuild friendships and routines.
