Mallo · Loneliness

An app for loneliness that checks in on you

Loneliness isn’t always about being physically alone—it’s often the feeling of not being seen or remembered. Mallo is designed as a low-pressure ritual: daily check-ins that come to you, persistent memory so the relationship deepens, and a weekly recap to reflect without judgment. Not therapy—a small, consistent thread.

How it works

  • Proactive daily check-ins—it reaches out, so you don’t have to initiate every time.
  • Persistent memory so it feels like a relationship that grows, not a blank chat.
  • Weekly recap to notice patterns and what mattered—gently.

Clear boundaries (not therapy)

Mallo is an AI companion app, not a substitute for professional support. If you’re in crisis or need mental health care, please reach out to a qualified professional. Mallo is designed as a daily ritual—someone who remembers you and checks in—not clinical treatment.
