Mallo · Remote work

An AI companion for remote work

Remote work can be calm and freeing—and also quietly isolating. When your day is mostly tabs, tasks, and Slack, it’s easy to lose the thread of how you’re actually doing. Mallo gives you a gentle check-in you can keep: proactive daily prompts, memory, and a weekly recap that helps you notice patterns.

What it’s good for

  • End-of-day decompression without “journaling pressure”.
  • Noticing drift (stress, sleep, motivation) before it becomes a spiral.
  • Continuity across weeks—so your reflections don’t reset every Monday.

How it works (in plain English)

  • Daily check-ins that don’t feel like journaling.
  • Persistent memory so you don’t start from zero every day.
  • Weekly recap that reflects patterns (wins, weight, threads).
